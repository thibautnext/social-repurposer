# Social Media Content Repurposer — Business Model & Go-to-Market Strategy

## Product Overview

**Social Repurposer** is a SaaS that transforms any article (URL or pasted text) into 10+ content variants optimized for different social media platforms:
- **Twitter** (10-tweet thread)
- **LinkedIn** (professional post)
- **TikTok** (viral script with hook)
- **Instagram** (3 captions)
- **Facebook** (engagement-focused post)
- **Email** (newsletter)

### Core Value Proposition
- **Save 80% of content creation time**: 1 article → 50+ pieces of content
- **AI-powered optimization**: Each platform gets contextually tuned content
- **Copy-to-clipboard ready**: No manual formatting needed
- **Bulk publish integration** (Phase 2): Schedule across platforms simultaneously

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

#### 1. **Freemium Subscription** (Primary)
- **Free Tier**: 5 articles/month
  - Basic variants (text only)
  - Community support
  - No integrations

- **Pro Tier**: $29/month
  - 100 articles/month
  - All variants + custom content types
  - Bulk publish ready
  - Email support
  - API access (rate-limited)

- **Enterprise Tier**: Custom pricing ($500+/month)
  - Unlimited articles
  - Custom AI models
  - Full API access
  - Dedicated account manager
  - On-premises option

#### 2. **Usage-Based Overage** (Secondary)
- Free tier: $0.10 per extra article
- Pro tier: Included in subscription
- Enterprise: Volume discount

#### 3. **Platform Integrations** (Phase 2)
- Connect directly to Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook
- Scheduled publishing (+$9.99/month add-on)
- Analytics dashboard (+$19.99/month add-on)

## Market Analysis

### Target Audience

1. **Content Creators & Influencers** (45% of market)
   - Pain point: Time spent creating variants for each platform
   - Avg. revenue per user: $35-50/month
   - Acquisition: TikTok, Twitter, ProductHunt

2. **Marketing Teams (SMB)** (35% of market)
   - Pain point: Lack of resources to repurpose content
   - Avg. revenue per user: $45-80/month
   - Acquisition: LinkedIn, B2B ads, content marketing

3. **Agencies & Consultants** (20% of market)
   - Pain point: Scaling content for multiple clients
   - Avg. revenue per user: $200+/month (team plans)
   - Acquisition: Enterprise sales, partnerships

### Market Size
- **Total Addressable Market (TAM)**: ~2M content creators + marketers
- **Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)**: ~200K English-speaking creators
- **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM Year 1)**: ~2,500 users (1.25% SAM)

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Launch (Month 0-2)
1. **ProductHunt Launch** (Day 1)
   - Craft compelling demo video
   - Target #1 spot with early community engagement
   - Expected reach: 500-1,000 upvotes, 10-15K visitors

2. **Community Building** (Week 1-4)
   - Twitter/X: 20 threads showcasing transformations
   - Reddit: r/SideProject, r/webdev, r/SaaS
   - LinkedIn: Articles on content repurposing ROI
   - Discord: Join creator communities, offer beta access

3. **Influencer Seeding** (Week 2-8)
   - Reach out to 20 content creators (2-10K followers each)
   - Offer free Pro tier for 3 months
   - Get testimonials + organic shares

### Phase 2: Growth (Month 2-6)
1. **Content Marketing**
   - Blog: "How to repurpose 1 article into 100 posts" (SEO)
   - Case studies: 3-5 creator success stories
   - Email course: 7-day "Content Repurposing Masterclass"

2. **Paid Acquisition**
   - Twitter ads: $2-3 CPC, target content creators
   - Reddit ads: Hyper-targeted subreddit campaigns
   - Google Ads: Long-tail content repurposing keywords
   - Aim for <$15 CAC

3. **Partnerships**
   - Medium, Hashnode: Integration + cross-promotion
   - Email platforms (Substack, Beehiiv): Feature integration
   - Notion: Content template marketplace

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 6+)
1. **B2B Expansion**
   - Target marketing agencies + SaaS companies
   - LinkedIn sales outreach
   - Free trial for teams (5+ seats)

2. **Platform Integrations**
   - Direct publishing to Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.
   - Bulk scheduling dashboard
   - Analytics tracking (engagement per variant)

3. **Enterprise Sales**
   - Warm outreach to Fortune 500 marketing teams
   - Custom deployment options
   - Revenue: $500-5,000/month per customer
